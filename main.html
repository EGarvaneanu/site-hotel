About us

Offering en suite rooms with balconies and views over Bucegi Mountains, Hotel Smart is 1.5 km away from Sinaia's centre. It features a sauna with massages and a gym.

Guests can have a barbecue on the covered terrace or sample a traditional Romanian meal at the cosy restaurant with wooden beams. There is a separate breakfast room and an open-air terrace in the garden of the Smart.

Sinaia's Train Station is 3 km away from Hotel Smart and free monitored parking is offered on site.
